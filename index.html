Hostel Rules & Regulations

A Few Important Rules To Follow

Following these rules is mandatory to maintain a peaceful and harmonious community.

1

Keep noise down after 10 PM — be mindful of neighbouring rooms.

2

Visitors are only allowed in the common room between 8 AM – 9 PM.

3

Smoking, alcohol, and illegal substances are strictly prohibited inside the hostel.

4

Inform the supervisor in advance if you need to enter after 10 PM.

5

Each resident is personally responsible for keeping their room clean.

6

Report any maintenance issue on the portal promptly — don't delay.

7

Monthly rent must be paid by the 5th of each month.

8

No pets are allowed anywhere on the hostel premises.

9

Changing rooms without admin approval is strictly not allowed.

10

Leave common areas clean after use.
